NEBU EXPO FOR GOLD & JEWELRY

"JEWEL OF SAUDI" is Kingdom of saudi Ardolds unique Business to Business Jewellery and Machinery Exhibition. The second edition of this exhibition will be held in Jeddah international Convention Center from 21st to 23rd NOV 2024. This exhibition aims to enhance the jewellery trade of Kingdom of Saudi Arabia by showcasing world class machinery, technology and Jewellery from around the globe and the potential benefits and opportunities associated with domestic production. The exhibition will pave the way for a more self-sufficient and prosperous jewellery industry in KSA. Through collective efforts and investments, we can build a thriving domestic manufacturing sector that aligns with the vision2030 initiative.
